About Us

Authentic Thai Flavors in Coventry

Welcome to Thai Grass! We're a family-run Thai street food kitchen bringing authentic flavors from the streets of Thailand right to the heart of Coventry.

Located inside Tokyo Express, we serve freshly prepared Thai dishes made with traditional recipes and quality ingredients. From our signature curries to our best-selling Sticky Chili Beef, every dish is cooked with passion and served with pride.

Whether you're vegan, looking for a hearty meal, or just craving authentic Thai street food, we've got something special waiting for you. Come visit us and experience the taste of Thailand!
